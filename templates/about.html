About Nordschleife Crash Data

This is the place for all racing simulator enthusiats to log their accidents when racing the Nordschleife, aka Green Hell.

In return, you get an interactive data visualisation of the circuit and the opportunity to find out:

  • Which corners are the most "dangerous"
  • How the sections of the track look like and how to approach them (mostly personal tips)
  • Backstory about the circuit and some of its sections
  • More to come!

What are you waiting for?! Click on a section to read more or to log your accident!

Note: some sections can be hard to click on. A better map will be made later.
